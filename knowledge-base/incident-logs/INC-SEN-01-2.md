# Incident INC-SEN-01-2

- **Date:** 2026-02-27
- **Shift:** Night shift
- **Reported by:** M. Suzuki
- **Equipment:** Line 7 — sensors
- **Related fault mode:** SEN-01 (Photoelectric sensor misalignment)
- **Status:** Resolved

## Reported symptom

> Part-present sensor at station 2 is flaky, sometimes it just doesn't see the panel at all.

## Root cause

Photoelectric part-present sensors on Line 7 are set to a tight alignment tolerance because of the short detection window at takt speed. Most false-stop complaints trace back to bracket misalignment after nearby manual adjustments, or lens fouling from the adjacent touch-up booth.

## Resolution

1. Realign the sensor bracket using the laser alignment jig and re-torque the mounting screw
2. Clean the lens with an approved lint-free wipe
3. Relocate any new reflective fixtures out of the sensor's field of view
