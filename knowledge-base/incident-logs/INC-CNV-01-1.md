# Incident INC-CNV-01-1

- **Date:** 2026-01-06
- **Shift:** Night shift
- **Reported by:** T. Yamamoto
- **Equipment:** Line 7 — conveyor mechanical
- **Related fault mode:** CNV-01 (Belt slippage from worn drive pulley)
- **Status:** Resolved

## Reported symptom

> Belt seems to be slipping on the drive pulley, panels are arriving late to station 3.

## Root cause

A mismatch between commanded VFD speed and actual measured belt speed, especially accompanied by a rhythmic squeal, points to drive pulley lagging wear. Re-lagging is a planned-maintenance item; in the interim, cleaning and re-tensioning can restore acceptable grip for one to two more shifts.

## Resolution

1. Clean the pulley surface with the approved degreaser if contamination is found
2. Re-tension the belt to spec
3. If lagging is worn smooth, schedule pulley re-lagging at next planned downtime
