## Reported symptom

> /Gearbox is running hotter than usual and there's a slightly odd smell near the drive end.

## Expert answer

This is a worn gearbox output-shaft seal. The odd smell is the gear oil starting to scorch as it seeps past the seal onto the housing, and the heat is from running low on oil. 1) Check the oil level/color at the sight glass -- if low or dark, that confirms it. 2) Replace the output-shaft seal at next planned downtime. 3) Top up with the spec gear oil (see maintenance manual) and monitor for a week. Don't run it dry -- escalate immediately if the smell gets stronger or oil level keeps dropping.
